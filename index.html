Program Summary

The program supports ~10 undergraduate students for a 10-week summer session. Students take short courses, attend topical lectures, work with faculty mentors, present weekly progress, and deliver final reports and presentations.

Participants build skills to tackle challenges in quality of service, heterogeneity, performance, mobility, security/privacy, and user experience. Alumni are encouraged to present their work at home institutions post-program.

Runs May–July. See Important Dates. Stipend, housing/meal support (if needed), and travel support are provided.
