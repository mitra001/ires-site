Highlights

  • The participants will get a stipend of $3000, room (if needed) and meal allowance, plus support for travel to/from the Site
  • Students will be equipped with essential research skills and industry-relevant experience
  • By engaging with complex, real-world use-inspired research problems, students will develop critical thinking, advanced problem-solving capabilities, and technical expertise (e.g., mathematical modeling, advanced machine learning), preparing them to become highly effective professionals
  • Graduate students will gain deeper proficiency in complex algorithms and methodologies
  • Undergraduates will build a robust research foundation, positioning them competitively for future academic or industry roles
  • Multi-cultural experience
  • Communication and presentation skills
  • International industry exposure
  • Interdisciplinary competencies and teamwork skills
